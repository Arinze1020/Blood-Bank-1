About

The software system is an online blood bank management system that helps in managing various blood bank operation effectively. The project consists of a central repository containing various blood deposits available along with associated details. These details include blood type,donor name,address and date of donation. These details help in maintaining and monitoring the blood deposits. The project is an online system that allows to check whether required blood deposits of a partcular group are available in the blood bank.
